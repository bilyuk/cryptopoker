# CryptoPoker Mission

## What We're Building

CryptoPoker is a web-first, crypto-native, provably fair P2P Texas Hold'em platform where hand outcomes and pot settlement are verifiable on-chain.

Core product direction:
- Trust-minimized gameplay anchored by smart contract settlement.
- Deterministic and auditable game flow.
- Security-first custody and session architecture.

## Who It's For

Primary users are crypto-native players who want trustless poker with transparent game integrity and on-chain verifiability.

## What MVP Done Looks Like

MVP is complete when:
- Two players can connect to a table and complete a full hand of Texas Hold'em.
- Card dealing and hand progression follow a provably fair flow.
- The winning pot is settled on-chain by smart contracts (source of truth).
- The flow is reproducible and auditable from game events and contract state.

## Non-Goals for MVP

- Tournaments
- Mobile app clients
- Fiat on-ramps/off-ramps
