# Vision

## Problem

Online poker players have limited trust in game fairness, opaque operator controls, and fragmented crypto onboarding.

## Mission

Build the most trusted crypto poker experience by combining provable fairness, secure asset flows, and competitive gameplay quality.

## Product Goals

- Deliver real-money crypto poker with fast table join and reliable gameplay.
- Provide public fairness verification for each hand.
- Maintain strong security controls around wallets, deposits, and withdrawals.
- Enable robust anti-collusion and anti-fraud protections.

## Non-Goals (Phase 1)

- Multi-game casino expansion.
- Cross-chain generalized wallet support.
- Complex social features beyond table chat and player identity basics.

## Success Metrics

- Table uptime and hand settlement reliability.
- Time-to-seat and deposit-to-play latency.
- Fraud incident rate and dispute resolution time.
- Player trust metrics tied to fairness verification adoption.
