# Invite Copy and Share Buttons Open Preview Instead of Copying

> *This was generated by AI during triage.*

Status: ready-for-agent

Category: bug

## What is broken

In the waiting room, the `Copy` and `Share` invite controls are wired to the invite preview navigation instead of copy/share behavior.

## Reproduction

1. Enter the app as a guest.
2. Create a private room.
3. In the waiting room, click `Copy`.

## Actual behavior

The app navigates to the invite preview screen.

## Expected behavior

`Copy` should copy the invite link to the clipboard and show a success/failure confirmation. `Share` should invoke a share flow where available, or degrade to copying the link with clear feedback.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] `Copy` writes the invite URL to the clipboard.
- [ ] `Copy` confirms success or failure in the UI.
- [ ] `Share` uses the browser share API where available or falls back to copy.
- [ ] Neither control navigates away from the waiting room unless that is explicitly exposed as a separate preview action.
